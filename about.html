ABOUT

PRAYAAG is the annual techno-cultural fest of LBS Institute of Technology for Women, a convergence of innovation, creativity, and engineering spirit. The fest serves as a platform for students to showcase technical excellence, artistic expression, and collaborative problem solving through competitions, workshops, and immersive experiences.
